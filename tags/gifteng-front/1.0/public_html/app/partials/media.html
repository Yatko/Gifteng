Overview

People want things they can’t afford, and others want to get rid of things easily and quickly. Gifteng solves these two problems while allowing users to build a new kind of social status based on a very unselfish thing: giving. The name combines “gift” and “engagement”. Gifteng users connect with one another through the act of giving. We endorse swapping instead of shopping.

The more a user gives, the more they can receive. And the larger the site’s user base grows, the better the experience will become.

How does it work?

  1. For each gift you post, you earn four requests.
  2. Posting takes less than a minute. All you need is a picture, a brief description and zip code.
  3. The gift becomes available for request at noon EST. Once it’s up, you’ll be able to share it with your friends via Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest.
  4. Up to three members in the Gifteng community can send requests for the gift. You select who will receive it.
  5. Use the private messaging tool to arrange pickup or mail the gift using a pre-paid shipping label paid for by the receiver.
